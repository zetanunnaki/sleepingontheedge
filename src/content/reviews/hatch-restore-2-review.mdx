The **Hatch Restore 2** is the rare product that made us want to put our phone in another room. After 90 nights, here's what we found.

<ProductCard productId="hatch-restore-2" badge="Editor's Choice" />

## What we liked

The wind-down routines are the killer feature. You build a sequence — say, 20 minutes of rain sounds, then a 5-minute meditation, then lights out — and the device handles it. No phone, no notifications, no doomscroll.

## What we didn't

The premium content sits behind a subscription. The hardware is fully functional without it, but the marketing nudges you constantly.
