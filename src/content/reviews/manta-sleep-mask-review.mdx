Most sleep masks are flat strips of fabric that smother your eyelids. The **Manta Sleep Mask** uses adjustable eye cups instead — and after 45 nights, it's the only mask we still reach for.

<ProductCard productId="manta-sleep-mask" badge="Editor's Choice" />

## What we liked

The eye cups create real darkness without touching your lashes. Side-sleeping? No pressure on the eyeballs. REM eye movement happens freely — no friction-induced wake-ups at 4 AM.

## What we didn't

It's bulkier than a flat mask. If you travel with a small toiletry kit, you'll feel it.
