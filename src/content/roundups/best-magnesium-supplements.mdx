Magnesium is the single most over-recommended sleep supplement on the internet — and most of what's sold is the wrong form, the wrong dose, or both. Here's what actually works.

## The forms that matter

Two forms of magnesium are well-supported for sleep:

- **Magnesium glycinate** — calming, GABA-active, gentle on the gut.
- **Magnesium L-threonate** — crosses the blood-brain barrier, supports memory consolidation during sleep.

Skip oxide and citrate for sleep — they're laxatives, not sedatives.
